Activity Point Tracker

The Activity Point Tracker is a web platform designed to simplify the evaluation and awarding of extracurricular activity points at the university. It uses OCR technology to extract key details from uploaded certificates and automatically assigns points based on a predefined system. The platform ensures accuracy through name verification and duplicate certificate prevention preventing misuse and aiding in certificate authentication. It also offers real-time point tracking for students and detailed reporting tools for faculty, making the entire process efficient and transparent.

OnClick

onClick is an innovative Android app that tackles food wastage and resource inequality by enabling easy donations and access to household services. It allows users to donate surplus items like food and clothing to those in need. The app also acts as a marketplace, connecting users with local professionals for services such as plumbing, electrical work, and domestic help. By bridging surplus and scarcity, onClick promotes efficient resource use and community support.
